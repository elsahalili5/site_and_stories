REGION:GJAKOVË

MUNICIPALITY:GJAKOVË

SITE: KRALAN

DATE:2-4 APRIL 1999

On April 2, 3 and 4, 1999, Serbian Police surrounded the village of Kralan/Kraljane in Gjakovë/Djakovica and gathered in one location about 1 thousand men and boys from this village and dozens from surrounding villages. They are kept there for two days without being allowed to move or eat. After two days, namely on April 4, these people are divided into two groups, one group is released, while the other group of about 90 persons disappears without a trace. From this group, 23 mutilated bodies are found and identified in a mass grave in Serbia near Lake Perucac, in the Municipality of Bajina Basta.
