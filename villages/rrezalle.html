REGION:MITROVICË

MUNICIPALITY:SKËNDERAJ

SITE: RREZALLË

DATE:5 APRIL 1999

On April 5, 1999, Serbian Police surrounded the village of Rezallë in Skënderaj, executing about 100 Albanian civilians. Initially, Serbian Police entered every Albanian house, looting valuables (money and gold) and then gathered all the inhabitants in the center of the village, proceeding to separate the men and the women, and ordering the men to go in two directions, only to have them all executed within a few minutes. Aiming to cover up the crime, Serbia returned to the crime scene a few weeks later and exhumed the bodies. 28 people killed in Rrezallë/Rezala (out of a total of 45 people killed) were found in a hidden mass grave in Rudnica, Municipality of Raska, in Serbia.
