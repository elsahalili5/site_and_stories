REGION:MITROVICË

MUNICIPALITY: MITROVICË

SITE: RRUGA E KOVAÇËVE

DATE:14 APRIL 1999

On April 14, 1999, Serbian Police entered several Albanian houses on Rruga e Kovaçëve (Blacksmith Street) in Mitrovicë/Kosovska Mitrovica and after the looting (taking of valuables), Serbian Police kick out of their homes 20 boys and men from this neighborhood and all of them are killed a few meters away from home.
