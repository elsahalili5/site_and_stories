MUNICIPALITY: VUSHTRRI

SITE:STUDIME

DATE:2 MAY 1999

On the night of May 2, 1999, over 180 people were killed by Serbia in the village of Studime/Sudimlja in Vushtrri/Vucitrn. Most of the victims were killed on a tractor on their way to Albania, while others were killed in their homes. All the victims were first robbed of up to 1,000 Deutsche Marks per person, then they were subjected to having Serbian state insignia carved on their bodies by knife, and in the end they were all executed at close range.
