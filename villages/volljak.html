REGION:PEJË

MUNICIPALITY:KLINË

SITE: VOLLJAK

DARE:2 APRIL 1999

On April 2, 1999, Serbian Police otherwise stationed at several checkpoints along the Klinë-Gjakovë road, deployed to the village of Volljak/Volujak and entered each Albanian house taking all valuables (money, gold jewelry, and other household items). In cases where families had nothing to give to the Serbian Police formations, the latter killed at least one person in those families. Thus, on that day, there were 10 people killed in this village.
