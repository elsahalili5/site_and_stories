DARK HERITAGE IN KOSOVO

This project is an effort to collect and preserve the memories and places of war crimes in Kosovo. This online platform aims to establish a ‘digital memory’ which presents in a dignified manner these tragic war experiences for all those who want to learn more about the dark heritage of Kosovo.
